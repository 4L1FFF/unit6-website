Our Facilities

Everything your dog needs for a comfortable, fun, and safe stay

🛁

Grooming Parlour & Spa

Pamper your pooch with professional care using natural, hypoallergenic products. Our experienced groomers provide a relaxing spa experience for dogs of all sizes and breeds.

Services Include:

  • ✓ Full-service bathing and blow-dry
  • ✓ Breed-specific haircuts and styling
  • ✓ Nail trimming and grinding
  • ✓ Ear cleaning and teeth brushing
  • ✓ Aromatherapy baths
  • ✓ Relaxing massage sessions
  • ✓ De-shedding treatments
  • ✓ Flea and tick baths

*All products are natural and hypoallergenic

🏠

Luxury Accommodation

5-star comfort for a perfect stay with home-like amenities. Each room is designed to make your dog feel comfortable and relaxed.

Room Features:

  • ✓ Underfloor heating for winter comfort
  • ✓ Underfloor cooling for hot Hong Kong days
  • ✓ Flat-screen TV with dog programming
  • ✓ Orthopedic memory foam beds
  • ✓ Webcam access (optional)
  • ✓ Individual climate control
  • ✓ Soundproof walls
  • ✓ Soft ambient lighting
🚗

Chauffeur Collect & Return

Door-to-door convenience with our complimentary pickup and drop-off service across Hong Kong Island.

Service Includes:

  • ✓ Free pickup and drop-off within Hong Kong Island
  • ✓ Air-conditioned, sanitized vehicles
  • ✓ Comfortable travel crates provided
  • ✓ Short journey with soothing music
  • ✓ Text confirmation when your dog is picked up
  • ✓ Real-time tracking available
🌳

Secure Outdoor Paddocks

Safe, spacious, and supervised outdoor play areas where dogs can run, play, and explore.

Features:

  • ✓ Double-gated entry for security
  • ✓ 6-foot high fencing
  • ✓ Artificial turf and natural grass areas
  • ✓ Shaded rest areas
  • ✓ Supervised group play
  • ✓ Separate areas for small and large dogs
  • ✓ Agility equipment available
🏥

24-Hour Veterinary Services

Peace of mind with around-the-clock veterinary care. Our on-call vets ensure your dog is always in safe hands.

Vet Services:

  • ✓ On-site vet available 24/7
  • ✓ Partnership with 24-hour emergency hospital
  • ✓ Regular health checks during stay
  • ✓ Medication administration (no extra fee)
  • ✓ Immediate emergency response
  • ✓ Daily health monitoring reports
