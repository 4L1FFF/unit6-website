Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for weekends and holidays. Weekdays can often be booked with 48 hours notice.

Can siblings share a room?

Yes! Dogs from the same household can share a room for a discounted rate. Contact us for pricing.

What if my dog needs medication?

Our 24-hour vet team can administer medications at no extra cost. Just provide clear instructions.

Is there a discount for long stays?

Yes! Ask about our extended stay packages for discounts on stays of 2 weeks or more.
